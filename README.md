# SCT_DA_04 – Business Insights Report (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a Marketing Campaign dataset to uncover customer behavior, spending patterns, demographic trends, and business insights that can help improve marketing strategies and ROI.

The analysis was carried out using **Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn** in Jupyter Notebook.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Clean and inspect the dataset
* Identify missing values and data quality issues
* Analyze customer demographics
* Explore income and age distributions
* Understand customer spending behavior
* Discover relationships between variables using correlation analysis
* Generate business recommendations based on findings

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

* Dataset: Marketing Campaign Dataset
* Records: 2240
* Features: 29

Key attributes include:

* Campaign Responses

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Most customers belong to the Graduation education category.
* Married customers form the largest customer segment.
* Income distribution is right-skewed with a few high-income outliers.
* Wine products account for the highest spending among all product categories.
* Higher-income customers generally spend more across product categories.
* Several spending variables show strong positive correlations with each other.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Allocate more marketing budget toward high-value customer segments.
* Promote wine and meat products through targeted campaigns.
* Develop personalized offers based on customer spending patterns.
* Focus retention strategies on customer groups with high purchasing potential.
